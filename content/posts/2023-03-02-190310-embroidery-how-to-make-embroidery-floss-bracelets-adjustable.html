How to Make Embroidery Floss Bracelets Adjustable

The next step is to add an adjustable closure. To do this, you will need to cut two pieces of floss that are the same length. Tie one end of each piece of floss to the end of the bracelet. Then, tie the other ends together in a knot. This will create a loop that can be adjusted to fit your wrist.
