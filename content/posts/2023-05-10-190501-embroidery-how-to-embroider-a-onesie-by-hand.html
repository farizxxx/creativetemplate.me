Embroidering a onesie by hand is a great way to add a personal touch to a special gift or to create a unique piece of clothing for yourself. It's a fun and creative activity that anyone can do with a few simple supplies and a bit of practice.

To get started, you'll need a onesie, embroidery floss, an embroidery hoop, a needle, and a pair of scissors. Begin by threading the needle with the embroidery floss and tying a knot at the end. Place the onesie in the embroidery hoop and tighten it so that the fabric is taut. Then, use the needle to stitch the design of your choice onto the onesie. You can use a variety of stitches, such as the running stitch, the back stitch, and the French knot. When you're finished, cut the thread and tie a knot at the end.

How to Embroider a Onesie by Hand

1. Gather supplies: onesie, embroidery floss, embroidery hoop, needle, and scissors.

2. Thread the needle with the embroidery floss and tie a knot at the end.

3. Place the onesie in the embroidery hoop and tighten it.

4. Use the needle to stitch the design of your choice onto the onesie.

5. Cut the thread and tie a knot at the end.
