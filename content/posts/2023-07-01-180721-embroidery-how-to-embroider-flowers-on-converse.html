Embroidering flowers on converse is a great way to add a unique and personal touch to your shoes. It's a fun and creative way to express yourself and make your shoes stand out from the crowd. With a few simple supplies and a bit of patience, you can easily create beautiful flower designs on your converse.

To get started, you'll need a pair of converse, embroidery thread, an embroidery needle, and a pair of scissors. Begin by cutting a length of thread that is twice as long as you want your design to be. Thread the needle and tie a knot at the end of the thread. Then, start stitching the flower design onto the converse. You can use a variety of stitches to create the design, such as the running stitch, the back stitch, and the French knot. Once you have finished stitching the design, tie a knot at the end of the thread to secure it.
