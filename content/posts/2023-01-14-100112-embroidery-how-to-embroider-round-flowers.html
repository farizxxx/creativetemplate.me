Embroidery is a great way to add a unique touch to any project. Round flowers are a classic motif that can be used to decorate clothing, accessories, and home decor items. With a few simple stitches, you can create beautiful and intricate designs that will last for years to come.

To begin, you will need a few basic supplies: embroidery floss, a needle, and a piece of fabric. Start by threading the needle with the embroidery floss. Then, tie a knot at the end of the thread. How to embroider round flowers will depend on the type of flower you are creating. For a simple daisy, start by making a small circle in the center of the flower. Then, make a series of small stitches around the circle to create the petals. Finally, add a few French knots in the center of the flower to complete the design.
