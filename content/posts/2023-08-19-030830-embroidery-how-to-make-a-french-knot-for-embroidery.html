Making a French knot is a great way to add texture and dimension to your embroidery projects. It is a simple stitch that can be used to create a variety of designs. With a few simple steps, you can easily learn how to make a French knot. Start by threading your needle with the desired color of embroidery floss. Then, wrap the floss around the needle twice and insert the needle into the fabric. Finally, pull the needle and floss through the fabric to create the knot. With practice, you can master the French knot and use it to create beautiful embroidery designs.
