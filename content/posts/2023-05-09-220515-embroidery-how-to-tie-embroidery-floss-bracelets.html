How to Tie Embroidery Floss Bracelets

First, you will need to gather your supplies. You will need embroidery floss, scissors, and a tape measure. You can also use a ruler if you don’t have a tape measure. Cut the embroidery floss into four equal lengths. The length of the floss will depend on the size of your wrist. Measure your wrist and add an extra inch or two for tying the bracelet.

Next, tie the four strands of floss together at one end. Make sure the knot is tight and secure. Separate the strands into two groups of two. Take the two strands in one group and tie them together in a knot. Repeat this step with the other two strands. This will create a pattern of two knots and two loops.

Now, you can start to braid the bracelet. Take the two strands on the left and cross them over the two strands on the right. Then, take the two strands on the right and cross them over the two strands on the left. Continue this pattern until you reach the desired length. Make sure to keep the braid tight and even.

Once you have reached the desired length, tie the four strands together at the end. Make sure the knot is tight and secure. Finally, trim the ends of the floss and you have a beautiful embroidery floss bracelet!
