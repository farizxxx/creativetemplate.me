To get started, you’ll need a few basic supplies: embroidery floss, a needle, and a piece of fabric. You can use any type of fabric you like, but a lightweight cotton fabric works best. Once you have your supplies, you’re ready to begin!

Start by threading your needle with the embroidery floss. You can use any color you like, but a light pink or red looks best for roses. Once your needle is threaded, you’re ready to start stitching.
