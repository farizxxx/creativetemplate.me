The first step in learning how to embroider hand towels is to choose the right fabric. Hand towels are usually made from cotton, linen, or a blend of the two. Cotton is the most common choice for embroidery, as it is easy to work with and the stitches will hold well. Once you’ve chosen the fabric, you’ll need to decide on the design. You can either create your own design or use a pre-made pattern. If you’re creating your own design, make sure to draw it out on paper first so you can get an idea of how it will look on the towel.

Once you’ve chosen the design, you’ll need to transfer it to the fabric. You can do this by tracing the design onto the fabric with a water-soluble pen or by using an embroidery transfer paper.
